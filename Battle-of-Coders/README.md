# **Track & Problem Statement**
## **Track 6: Dynamic Night Market Navigator** 
Problem Statement: Tourists and locals frequently miss out on Melaka’s vibrant night markets because schedules and vendor locations change often and are not available in a central system. This lack of visibility results in lower foot traffic for vendors and missed opportunities for the local community to support small businesses.

## 🔹 **Project Overview**
PASAR-SMART is a centralized digital management system designed to boost the visibility of Melaka’s night markets. By tracking changing schedules and vendor locations across districts like Alor Gajah, Jasin, Melaka Tengah, Bukit Beruang and Ayer Keroh the system provides a clear overview for the community. It ensures that moving markets are updated in real-time, helping to drive foot traffic to local vendors and boost sales.

### ✨ **Key Features**
👤 **Vendor & Admin Management**:A CRUD-based module to organize participant data and stall types.

📍 **Market Schedule Tracker**:Logs weekly locations and specific stall assignments to ensure real-time accuracy.

📅 **Live Market Calendar**:Provides a clear overview of active trading hubs for community access.

📊 **Insightful Reporting**:Processes data to provide business insights via downloadable Word documents.

🔍 **SQL-Driven Analytics**:Uses JOIN and GROUP BY operations at the database level to aggregate vendor data.

🛡️ **Error Handling**:Includes validations to prevent crashes from negative inputs or past dates.
